# contact-cta-guard

## When to use
- Any layout, section, or copy change on the home page or contact areas

## Goals
- Preserve visibility of phone/email/CTA/map links
- Avoid accidental removal or hiding on mobile

## Workflow
1. Identify where contact/CTA/map links are rendered.
2. After changes, confirm elements remain present and visible.
3. Verify labels remain clear and trustworthy (no aggressive copy).

## Files to check
- `src/components/sections/Contact*`
- `src/components/layout/**`
- `src/app/page.tsx`

## Output
- Confirmation of preserved contact/CTA/map elements.
